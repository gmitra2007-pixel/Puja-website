About

Welcome to our Website 🙏 Our puja is a celebration of devotion, culture, and togetherness. Every year, we organize this festival with great enthusiasm to honor Maa Jagadhatri and keep our traditions alive.

From beautiful decorations and lighting to rituals, bhog, and cultural programs, our puja brings the entire community together. It is not just a festival, but a moment of joy, unity, and spiritual connection.

We warmly invite everyone to visit, take part in the celebrations, and experience the divine atmosphere with us. ✨
